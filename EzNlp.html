Ez-NLP

Ez-NLP Components

NLP enabled Data UI

Ez-NLP has a out of box NLP enabled Data access UI. The google like search box allows users typing questions regarding the interesting domain. The Ez-NLP's NLP engine processes the questions to understand data requests within specific domain. The questions are turned into a serials of service calls to the backing service providers. The results of the service calls are aggregated into the final results which are presented back to end users with relevant ranks.

The NLP engine can be trained to use with different business domain. The default domain in Ez-NLP is the financial domain especially wealth managemnet.

The NLP enaled Data UI can be used with voice recognition and mobile devices.

Advanced NLG

A subset of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Natural Language Generation (NLG) creates automated, data-driven communications. In its simplest form, NLG turns structured data into text. Advanced NLG, the AI software behind Ez-NLP, uses your intent as its guide to deliver the information you care most about. Applying advanced analytics to assess the data, it automatically transforms the resulting insights into human-sounding narratives at scale

This component is useful for generating research reports.
